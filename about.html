Our Story

It all began in October 2014, when two of Philly's most popular barbershop owners teamed up to bring you a Haircut Club. Block 59 is committed to providing customers with the highest quality haircuts at an affordable price.

At Block 59 Barbershop, we pride ourselves on taking a calm and mature approach to men's grooming. We understand that barbering is basically an art, a method of creative expression - it's about taking the ordinary and creating extraordinary.
